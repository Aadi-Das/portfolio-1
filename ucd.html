Learning Outcomes

These are the following learning outcomes that I believe I achieved in this class:

Interactive Media

In this class we worked on understanding our users and what they want from you. When creating prototype we created multiple iterations and improve on each version. We also had to conduct user research to figure out what users want and what reactions to users have to certain choices we make. We di usability testing as well as ab testing and incorporated that feedback to improve for the next iteration.

Design

What good design is, is subjective. Through this class I have learned how certain choices effect what we want to put out. And how the gestalt principles can be used when we make our choices. Users will always be able to spot bad design and user tend yo be creatures of habit so people have certain notions that we can put to use in our design. For example between a red and green button a user will automatically associate the red with delete or exit while green with complete or success.

Research

Research is constantly being done to understand users. Research is being done to understand users and their needs and wants. Research has to be done to stand out after knowing the industry and finding a niche. We had to research marketing strategies to understand how to entice and manipulate users.
