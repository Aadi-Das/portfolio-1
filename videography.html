Assignments

Videography is the last assignment to conclude this year and semester. I felt two ways about this assignment. We first came up with ideas, we ended up using Fleur's idea of creating a documentary of her dog. Melany and I each created a storyboard. Melany then created a high version fidelity of her story board. The recording part, I really enjoyed though i may have not gotten the best shots it was quite fun to try. Then the editing aspect was ome thing that I found especially tough. I will say that this was because of a lack of experiences using editing apps. Melany and I worked on editing this together with Melany compiling all the clips, I added transitions. I used the ken burn effect on the initial photos after we got feedback and Melany added more. I did the voiceover. Which Melany edited into the video. Fleur created the transcription and subtitles that we placed into the final video. Vanessa added breaks to the interview however her file could not be rendered so Melany and I redid them, but we used her idea of where clips could be added.Fleur was our actor and of course the main star, her dog, Timon. Towards the end, when i finally got the hang of using Davinchi I will say i enjoyed it more. I am happy with the end product considering no one in our group knew anything about videography and editing.
